    How to Get an English Medical Certificate in Busan (영문 진단서)

    2026-03-30

    If you're a foreigner living in or visiting Busan, there are situations where you need an official medical document written in English. In Korea, this is called a 영문 진단서 (yeongmun jindanseo) — an English medical certificate.

    This guide explains what it is, when you need one, and how to get one quickly in Busan.

    What Is an English Medical Certificate?

    An English medical certificate is an official document issued by a licensed Korean doctor. It states your diagnosis, treatment details, and medical condition — all written in English with the doctor's signature and clinic stamp.

    It is a legally recognized medical document that can be used internationally.

    When Do You Need One?

    You might need an English medical certificate for:

    • Visa applications — some embassies require proof of medical conditions or fitness
    • Embassy submissions — documentation for consular services
    • Insurance claims — foreign insurance companies need English documentation
    • Employer requests — your company abroad may require medical proof for sick leave
    • University enrollment — some schools require health clearance in English
    • Legal matters — court or immigration proceedings that need medical evidence

    How to Get One at Kim Joo-in Internal Medicine (Seomyeon)

    Getting an English medical certificate at our clinic is straightforward:

    1. Walk in — no appointment necessary
    2. Bring your passport — we need it for your official name and details
    3. Tell the front desk you need an English medical certificate (영문 진단서)
    4. See the doctor — Dr. Kim can conduct the consultation and discuss your needs in basic English
    5. Receive your certificate — typically issued the same day

    The entire process usually takes under 30 minutes.

    What's Included in the Certificate?

    Your English medical certificate will include:

    • Patient name (as shown on passport)
    • Date of birth
    • Date of examination
    • Diagnosis in English medical terminology
    • Treatment details (if applicable)
    • Doctor's signature and medical license number
    • Official clinic stamp
    • Clinic contact information

    Practical Tips

    • Cost: The certificate fee is affordable. Call ahead if you want to confirm the exact price.
    • Copies: Let us know if you need multiple originals — we can prepare them during your visit.
    • Timing: Same-day issuance is standard for most cases.
    • Language: The certificate is written entirely in English. A Korean version can be provided alongside if needed.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where can I get an English medical certificate in Busan?

    Kim Joo-in Internal Medicine Clinic in Seomyeon issues English medical certificates for foreigners. The clinic is a 5-minute walk from Seomyeon Station Exit 7 and offers walk-in service.

    How much does an English medical certificate cost in Korea?

    Kim Joo-in Internal Medicine issues English medical certificates. Fees depend on type and complexity. Visa medical exams are priced separately. Please contact the clinic at 051-802-7550 for current rates.

    How long does it take to get an English medical certificate?

    Most simple certificates (sick leave, fitness-to-work) are issued the same day. Complex certificates requiring tests may take 1-2 days.

    What types of English medical certificates can be issued?

    Common types include sick leave certificates, fitness-to-work certificates, visa medical exams, insurance claim forms, travel medical certificates, and school exemption letters.

    Will my insurance accept a Korean English medical certificate?

    Most international insurance companies accept Korean medical certificates issued in English. Check with your insurer for any specific format requirements.
