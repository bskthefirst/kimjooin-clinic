    Health Checkups in Busan for Foreigners: Everything You Need to Know

    2026-03-26

    Korean clinics offer world-class checkups at a fraction of Western prices. Advanced imaging, blood panels, endoscopy, and cardiac screening — all under one roof, often same-day results.

    English-Language Checkups at Kim Joo-in Internal Medicine

    Dr. Kim Joo-in, a 14-year professor at Busan Paik Hospital and former fellow at Washington University in St. Louis & Barnes-Jewish Hospital, conducts checkups with full English consultation.

    Basic Checkup

    • Blood pressure, height, weight, BMI
    • Blood tests (CBC, lipid panel, liver function, kidney function, blood sugar)
    • Urinalysis & ECG
    • Chest X-ray consultation

    Advanced Options

    • Gastroscopy — stomach & esophagus endoscopy
    • Abdominal ultrasound — liver, gallbladder, pancreas, kidneys
    • Thyroid & Cardiac ultrasound
    • Pulmonary function test — essential for smokers, asthma/COPD patients

    How to Prepare

    • Fast 8 hours if you want blood glucose & gastroscopy
    • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before
    • Bring your passport

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How much does a health checkup cost in Busan for foreigners?

    Kim Joo-in Internal Medicine offers health checkup packages. Costs depend on tests included. NHIS members may receive discounted or free annual checkups. Please contact the clinic at 051-802-7550 for current pricing.

    Do I need to fast before a Korean health checkup?

    Yes, fasting for 8-12 hours before your checkup is recommended for accurate blood test and abdominal ultrasound results. Water is fine.

    How long does a Korean health checkup take?

    Most general checkups take 1-2 hours, including blood tests, urine tests, X-ray, EKG, and consultation. Endoscopy adds 30-60 minutes.

    Can I get a health checkup in English in Busan?

    Yes. Kim Joo-in Internal Medicine in Seomyeon offers English-language consultations and can provide reports in English upon request.

    When can I get my checkup results?

    Most blood and urine results are available within 1-2 days. Imaging and biopsy results take 3-7 days. The clinic provides written reports and can email PDFs.
